About MLTelect Samoa

Your trusted electrical engineering partner in Samoa since 2009

Our Story

Founded in 2009, MLTelect Samoa has grown from a small electrical service provider to one of Samoa's most trusted electrical engineering and consultancy firms. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide safe, reliable, and innovative electrical solutions to homes and businesses across Samoa.

Over the years, we've successfully completed over 500 projects, ranging from residential electrical installations to complex industrial systems. Our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as Samoa's premier electrical services provider.
